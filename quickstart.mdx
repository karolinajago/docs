Ordering a property valuation with Estify AI takes about 15 minutes from opening the form to completing payment. You don't need an account, an appointment, or any real estate expertise — just the key details about the property you want valued. This guide walks you through every step, from what to prepare to what to do with your report once it arrives.

## Before You Start: What to Have Ready

Gathering the following information before you open the form makes the process faster and ensures your valuation is as accurate as possible. Most of these details are on the property listing, in your purchase agreement, or in the building's official documentation.

**Essential details:**
- Full property address (street, building number, apartment number, city, postal code)
- Total floor area in square metres (m²)
- Number of rooms (excluding kitchen and bathrooms)
- Floor number the property is on, and the total number of floors in the building
- Building type (e.g. block of flats, tenement building, detached house, semi-detached)
- Year of construction
- Current condition (new / good condition / requires renovation)
- List of amenities (balcony, terrace, garden, basement storage, lift, air conditioning)
- Primary window orientation (north, south, east, west, or mixed)
- Parking situation (underground garage space, surface parking, or none)
- Heating type (district heating, gas central heating, electric, or other)

**Highly recommended:**
- Land and Mortgage Register number (numer Księgi Wieczystej)

<Tip>
  Track down your Land and Mortgage Register (Księga Wieczysta) number before you start. It's usually on your purchase agreement, notarial deed, or mortgage documents — or you can find it at [ekw.ms.gov.pl](https://ekw.ms.gov.pl) by searching the property address. Including it allows Estify AI to run a full legal check for encumbrances, mortgages, and ownership status, which adds significant value to your report.
</Tip>

## Order Your Valuation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Estify AI">
    Open your browser and navigate to [estify.ai](https://estify.ai). The homepage shows current pricing and example report sections. When you're ready, click the **"Get valuation"** button — it's visible in the navigation bar and in the main hero section of the page.

    You don't need to create an account or log in. Your report will be delivered directly to the email address you provide during checkout.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill In the Property Form">
    The form is divided into clearly labelled sections covering location, building details, apartment specifics, amenities, and legal information. Work through each section in order — every field includes a short description to guide you.

    The form takes approximately **15 minutes** to complete for a standard apartment. Detached houses or properties with more complex ownership structures may take slightly longer.

    A few tips for filling in the form accurately:

    - **Floor area** — Use the net usable area (powierzchnia użytkowa), not the gross area, if both figures are available.
    - **Condition** — Be honest about the condition. Overrating a property's condition is one of the most common causes of valuation discrepancies.
    - **Amenities** — Include everything, even small items. Each amenity is factored individually into the model.
    - **Land Register number** — Enter it in the standard Polish format: four letters, a forward slash, a sequence of digits, and a check digit (e.g. `WA1M/00123456/7`).

    Once you've completed all sections, review your entries on the summary screen and click **"Continue to plan selection"**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Your Plan">
    You'll be presented with two report options:

    **Basic — 19 PLN** *(promotional price, regular 49 PLN)*
    Covers the AI valuation, appraiser verification, market analysis, comparable transactions, and a legal register check. Ideal for buyers and sellers who need a reliable price reference quickly.

    **Pro — 199 PLN** *(promotional price, regular 699 PLN)*
    Everything in Basic, plus investment metrics, rental yield estimates, 12–36 month price forecasts, environmental and location risk analysis, and a full narrative appraisal document. Recommended for mortgage applications, investment decisions, and negotiations requiring detailed documentation.

    Not sure which to choose? See the full feature comparison at [/reports/overview](/reports/overview).

    <Note>
      Promotional pricing is available for a limited time. The price shown at checkout is the price you pay — there are no hidden fees, subscription charges, or automatic renewals.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pay Securely">
    Estify AI supports all major payment methods used in Poland. Choose whichever is most convenient:

    - **BLIK** — Enter your 6-digit BLIK code from your mobile banking app for instant payment.
    - **Bank transfer** — Pay by standard or express transfer from any Polish bank account.
    - **Credit or debit card** — Visa and Mastercard accepted.
    - **Apple Pay** — Available on Safari on iPhone and Mac.
    - **Google Pay** — Available on Chrome and Android devices.
    - **PayPal** — Log in with your PayPal account for a quick checkout.
    - **Installments via Tpay** — Split the cost into monthly installments, available on Pro plan orders.

    All payments are processed securely. Estify AI does not store your card details.

    After payment, you'll be redirected to a confirmation screen and receive an **order confirmation email** within a few minutes. This email includes your order reference number and your expected report delivery date.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Your Report by Email">
    Your completed, appraiser-verified report will be delivered to the email address you provided **by the end of the next business day**. The email will include:

    - Your full valuation report as a PDF attachment
    - A summary of the key valuation figures
    - Guidance notes on how to read and use the report
    - Contact details if you have questions about the findings

    Check your spam or junk folder if the report hasn't arrived by end of business the following day. If you still haven't received it, contact Estify AI support with your order reference number.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to Do With Your Report

Once your report arrives, here's how to put it to work:

**For buyers and sellers — use it in price negotiations.** Your report gives you a certified, appraiser-backed valuation figure you can reference directly in negotiation. Sellers can justify their asking price; buyers can push back on inflated listings with independent data rather than opinions.

**For mortgage applications — submit it to your bank.** Banks and mortgage advisors routinely ask for independent property valuations during the credit assessment process. A Pro report, with its full narrative appraisal document, is formatted to meet these requirements.

**For investors — model your returns.** The investment metrics and price forecast sections in the Pro report give you the inputs you need for a straightforward ROI calculation: current value, expected appreciation, and estimated rental yield. Use these figures in your investment model before committing to a purchase.

**For agents — share it with your client.** If you're an agent who ordered the report on behalf of a client, forward the PDF directly. The report is written to be understood by property buyers and sellers without specialist knowledge.

<Tip>
  The valuation in your report reflects market conditions at the time of your form submission. If you're planning to use the report several months after it was issued — for a delayed sale or a refinancing conversation, for instance — consider ordering a fresh valuation to ensure the figures reflect current market data.
</Tip>
