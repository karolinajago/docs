## About Estify AI

## The Valuation Form

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What information do I need to fill in the form?">
    The form contains approximately 30 fields covering the essential characteristics of your property. You'll need details such as the property's address, type (apartment, house, commercial unit, etc.), total area, floor number, year of construction, current condition, and any notable features like a parking space, balcony, or garden. The more accurately you complete the form, the more precise your valuation will be — but you don't need any specialist knowledge to fill it in.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need the Land Register (Księga Wieczysta) number?">
    The Land Register (Księga Wieczysta) number is **optional** — you can submit the form without it. However, providing it significantly enhances the legal verification section of your report, including checks on ownership status, mortgages, encumbrances, and easements. If you have the number available, we strongly recommend including it. You can usually find it on your property deed, notarial act, or by searching the Ministry of Justice's electronic Księga Wieczysta portal.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does the form take to complete?">
    Most users complete the form in **5 to 10 minutes**. If you have your property details handy — such as the area in square metres, floor number, and year of construction — the process is very straightforward. The form uses smart autocomplete for addresses and guides you step by step, so you won't need to navigate complicated menus or look up technical terms.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I order a valuation for any property type?">
    Yes — Estify AI supports a wide range of property types, including apartments, houses, commercial premises, plots, and investment properties across Poland. Our models are trained on transaction data for each property category, so whether you're valuing a city-centre flat or a suburban building plot, the analysis will be relevant and accurate. If you're unsure whether your property type is supported, feel free to contact us before ordering.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Using Your Report

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I use the Estify report in a bank or mortgage discussion?">
    Estify AI reports are data-rich and prepared with appraiser oversight, making them a powerful reference point in negotiations and financial planning conversations. However, Polish banks typically require a formal appraisal (operat szacunkowy) prepared by a licensed real estate appraiser for mortgage underwriting purposes. The Estify Pro report is ideal for informing your own position, validating a bank's valuation, or supporting price negotiations — but you should confirm with your bank whether they accept third-party valuations before relying solely on the report for a mortgage application.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I interpret the price forecast?">
    The Pro report includes price forecasts for your property's estimated value over **up to 20 quarters** (5 years) into the future. Each forecast period shows a projected price range based on historical trends, macroeconomic indicators, local supply and demand data, and planned infrastructure developments in the area. The forecast is presented as a confidence band — a wider band indicates greater uncertainty (common for longer horizons), while a narrower band reflects stronger predictability. Use the forecast as a strategic planning tool, not as a guaranteed outcome.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long is the report valid?">
    An Estify AI report reflects market conditions at the time of the valuation. Property markets can shift due to interest rate changes, new developments, or broader economic events, so we recommend treating the report as current for **3 to 6 months** under stable market conditions. If significant time has passed or the market has moved materially, ordering a fresh valuation will give you the most accurate picture. Reordering is quick and affordable — your previously submitted form details are saved in your account to speed up the process.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
