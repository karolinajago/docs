## Get your valuation in 3 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fill in the property form">
    Enter approximately 30 details about the property — address, floor area, number of rooms, building type, condition, amenities, and more. The form takes around 15 minutes to complete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI processes your data">
    Estify's machine learning algorithms cross-reference your property details against data from 76 sources, including market transactions, spatial plans, legal registers, and macroeconomic indicators.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your report">
    Your completed report — verified by a certified appraiser — is delivered to your email the next business day. Pro reports include full charts, extended forecasts, and a decision-support tool.
  </Step>
</Steps>
