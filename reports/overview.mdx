## Which Plan Is Right for You?

**Choose Basic if you:**
- Want a fast, affordable snapshot of a property's market value
- Are in the early stages of researching a purchase or sale
- Need to quickly sense-check an asking price before committing further
- Are comparing multiple properties and want cost-effective valuations for each

**Choose Pro if you:**
- Are making a final decision on buying, selling, or investing
- Need detailed charts, forecasts, and investment metrics to justify your decision
- Want to use the report in price negotiations or discussions with a bank or agent
- Are an investor who needs ROI, ROE, and sensitivity analysis
- Represent a client and need a presentation-ready, comprehensive document

<Note>
The Pro plan is Estify AI's most popular option. At 199 PLN (promotional price), it costs a fraction of a traditional Polish property appraisal (*operat szacunkowy*), which typically runs ~1,500 PLN — and delivers more comprehensive data.
</Note>
