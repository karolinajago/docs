## What the Pro Report Includes

### Full Comprehensive Charts

Visualisations of price trends, market dynamics, comparable transactions, and all forecast scenarios. Charts make the data easier to interpret and share with others — whether a co-buyer, an advisor, or a bank.

### Extended Competition Analysis

A detailed breakdown of comparable properties currently on the market and recently sold, including price-per-square-metre analysis, days-on-market data, and positioning insights that tell you exactly how the subject property compares.

### Extended Location & Spatial Analysis

A thorough assessment of the property's location, covering transport links, walkability, proximity to schools, healthcare, commercial services and green spaces, as well as zoning plan analysis and the spatial development outlook for the surrounding area.

### Extended Investment Analysis

The Pro report goes deep on investment metrics so you can model your returns with confidence:

- **ROI (Return on Investment)** — total return relative to purchase cost
- **ROE (Return on Equity)** — return on the capital you put in
- **Sensitivity analysis** — how returns change under different price and rental assumptions
- **Financial leverage analysis** — the effect of mortgage financing on your equity returns
- **Revenue and cost breakdown** — projected rental income, maintenance costs, and net cash flow

### Extended Legal Verification

A comprehensive check of the property's legal status, including a full **Księga Wieczysta** (Polish land register) verification. This surfaces encumbrances, mortgages, easements, ownership disputes, and any other issues that could affect the property's value or your ability to transact freely.

### Price Forecasts Up to 20 Quarters

Short-, medium-, and long-range price projections spanning up to five years (20 quarters), modelled across multiple macroeconomic scenarios. You'll see optimistic, baseline, and pessimistic outlooks so you can plan for a range of market conditions.

### Environmental Risk Assessment

An evaluation of environmental factors that may affect the property's value or liveability, including flood zone status, air quality indices, noise levels, and proximity to industrial sites or protected areas.

### Neighborhood Profile

A data-driven portrait of the local community: demographic trends, infrastructure investment, crime statistics, and the overall socioeconomic trajectory of the area — essential context for both lifestyle buyers and investors.

### Decision-Support Tool

The Pro report includes an interactive decision-support tool that helps you translate the report's data into a concrete course of action. Input your own assumptions — target price, financing structure, hold period — and see how different scenarios play out. It's designed to help you answer the single most important question: should you buy, sell, hold, or negotiate?

### Verified by a Certified Appraiser

Every Pro report is reviewed and verified by a licensed property appraiser before it reaches your inbox. This gives the report independent credibility you can cite in negotiations, bank meetings, or legal proceedings.
