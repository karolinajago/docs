## Who the Basic Report Is For

The Basic report is ideal when you need a reliable valuation quickly and don't yet require deep investment metrics or multi-year forecasts. It suits:

- **Buyers** who want to verify whether an asking price is realistic before scheduling a viewing or making an offer
- **Sellers** looking for a data-backed starting point when setting a listing price
- **Anyone doing preliminary research** across several properties — the Basic plan's low cost makes it practical to run valuations on multiple options
- **Renters and landlords** who want a quick market-rate reference

## What the Basic Report Covers

<Steps>
  <Step title="Property Valuation">
    An AI-calculated market value estimate accurate to within 2.3%, verified by a certified appraiser and based on real transaction data from 76 integrated sources.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Market Data Summary">
    A concise overview of current market conditions in your property's local area, including recent comparable transactions and average price-per-square-metre benchmarks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Basic Competition Analysis">
    An assessment of similar properties currently listed or recently sold nearby, giving you context for where your property sits in the local market.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Basic Location & Spatial Analysis">
    A review of key location factors: proximity to public transport, schools, services, and green spaces, along with a summary of the surrounding area's characteristics.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Basic Legal Verification">
    A check of the property's legal status, flagging any notable issues that may affect its value or saleability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Basic Investment Indicators">
    Essential metrics to give you a sense of the property's investment potential, including a basic yield estimate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Basic Price Forecasts">
    A short-term price outlook for the local market, helping you understand whether now is a good time to buy, sell, or wait.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's Not Included in the Basic Report

The Basic plan is intentionally concise. If you need any of the following, consider upgrading to [Pro](/reports/pro):

- **Full charts and visualisations** — detailed graphs of price trends, market dynamics, and forecast scenarios
- **Extended competition, location, and investment analysis** — deeper breakdowns including ROI, ROE, sensitivity analysis, financial leverage, and revenue/cost modelling
- **20-quarter price forecasts with macroeconomic scenarios** — long-range projections across multiple economic conditions
- **Environmental risk assessment** — flood, air quality, and other environmental factors
- **Extended legal analysis** — full Księga Wieczysta (land register) verification
- **Decision-support tool** — an interactive tool that helps you model buying, selling, or investment decisions based on your report data
