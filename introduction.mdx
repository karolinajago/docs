Estify AI gives you a certified, data-backed property valuation for the Polish real estate market — without the week-long wait or the steep fees of a traditional appraisal. You fill in a short online form, our machine learning engine analyses your property against 76 data sources, and a verified report lands in your inbox the next business day. Whether you're buying, selling, investing, or advising clients, Estify AI puts authoritative numbers behind every decision.

## Why Estify AI

The Polish real estate market moves fast, and gut-feel estimates cost money. Estify AI was built to close the gap between guesswork and professional-grade valuation — at a fraction of the cost and in a fraction of the time.

### What Sets Estify Apart

- **2.3% accuracy** — Our AI model is trained and continuously refined in collaboration with the Cracow University of Economics, achieving valuations accurate to within 2.3% of actual market value.
- **76 data sources** — Every valuation draws on market transaction records, Land and Mortgage Register data, spatial planning documents, macroeconomic indicators, and local infrastructure data.
- **Appraiser verification** — Every report is reviewed and signed off by a certified real estate appraiser before delivery, giving your valuation legal and professional weight.
- **Next-business-day delivery** — Submit your form today; your report arrives by email tomorrow. No appointments, no site visits, no delays.

<Note>
  Estify AI valuations are designed for the Polish real estate market and are denominated in Polish złoty (PLN). All data sources, legal registers, and market comparables are Poland-specific.
</Note>

## Who Estify AI Is For

Estify AI is built for anyone who needs a reliable property value — fast.

- **Buyers** — Know exactly what a property is worth before you make an offer or apply for a mortgage. Walk into negotiations with certified data, not assumptions.
- **Sellers** — Price your property accurately from day one. Avoid leaving money on the table with an undervalued listing, or scaring buyers off with an inflated ask.
- **Investors** — Evaluate potential acquisitions in minutes. Access investment metrics, price forecasts, and rental yield estimates to stress-test your numbers before committing capital.
- **Real estate agents** — Offer clients a professional, appraiser-backed valuation as part of your service. Strengthen trust, speed up decisions, and differentiate your practice.

## What's Inside a Report

Every Estify AI report goes beyond a single price estimate. You receive a structured, multi-section document covering:

- **Market analysis** — Current price trends in the property's micro-market, with comparable recent transactions.
- **Competitive landscape** — How the property compares to active listings and recent sales in the same area.
- **Legal verification** — A check of Land and Mortgage Register (Księga Wieczysta) entries for encumbrances, easements, and ownership status.
- **Investment metrics** — Estimated rental yield, gross return on investment, and price-per-square-metre benchmarks.
- **Price forecasts** — Short- and medium-term valuation trajectories based on macroeconomic and local market trends.
- **Environmental and location risks** — Flood zone status, noise exposure, proximity to infrastructure, and other location-based risk factors.
