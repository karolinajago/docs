## The Problem: Clients Need Evidence, Not Opinions

Whether you're working with a seller who wants to maximize their price or a buyer who needs to know they're not overpaying, the fundamental challenge is the same: your clients need objective, credible data — and they need it quickly.

Common friction points agents face without that data:

- **Sellers who resist realistic pricing** because they're anchored to an emotional number rather than market evidence
- **Buyers who demand price reductions** you can't counter effectively without independent valuation data
- **Deals that stall or fall through** because one party doesn't trust the other's pricing claims
- **Hours spent on manual research** compiling comparable sales, zoning information, and neighborhood data that could be automated
- **Competing against agents** who show up to listing appointments with professional analysis tools

Estify solves all of these by giving you a certified, comprehensive report you can deliver in a fraction of the time it would take to compile manually.

## How Estify AI Helps Agents

Estify generates a full property report by the next business day. You (or your client) complete a ~30-field form in about 15 minutes. The AI processes data from 76 sources and a certified appraiser verifies the result.

The outcome is a professional document you can present to clients on both sides of a transaction, with:

- An **independent, appraiser-verified valuation** that carries more weight than an agent's estimate alone
- A **competition analysis** showing exactly where a property sits relative to current listings and recent sales
- **Legal verification** of the Księga Wieczysta — the kind of due diligence that builds client trust
- **Investment analysis** with ROI, ROE, and financial indicators for investor clients
- **20-quarter price forecasts** with macroeconomic scenarios for long-term context
- A **neighborhood profile** and **location analysis** that help clients visualize the living environment

## Your Step-by-Step Agent Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive Your Listing or Buyer Mandate">
    As soon as you take on a new client — whether a seller listing a property or a buyer looking to acquire — identify the target property and prepare to order an Estify report as part of your standard onboarding process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order the Estify Pro Report">
    Complete the online form with the property's details. At around 15 minutes of input time, it's significantly faster than manual research. Choose the **Pro plan** to get the full report — including forecasts, investment analysis, and extended charts — that justifies professional use.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Present the Report to Your Client">
    When the report arrives the next business day, walk your client through the key sections relevant to their situation. For sellers: lead with the valuation and competition analysis. For buyers: lead with the valuation gap and legal verification. For investors: lead with the investment analysis and 20-quarter forecast.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the Report in Client Negotiations">
    When you're representing a seller in negotiations, the Estify report is your primary evidence tool. When representing a buyer, it's your justification for a lower offer. In both cases, you're no longer relying on opinion — you're citing a certified, appraiser-verified document.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Support Bank and Mortgage Discussions">
    For clients pursuing mortgage financing, share the relevant report sections — particularly the valuation, legal verification, and investment analysis — with their mortgage advisor or bank. The appraiser-verified valuation can accelerate the credit assessment process and strengthen your client's financing application.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for Agents

<Tip>
  Order your Estify report **before your listing appointment**, not after. Arriving at a seller meeting with a certified valuation already in hand positions you as prepared, data-driven, and worth hiring. It also removes the awkward negotiation about asking price before you've even signed the mandate.
</Tip>

<Note>
  The Estify Pro report is designed to be shared. Consider including relevant pages — the valuation summary, competition analysis, and price forecast — directly in your buyer or seller presentation decks. For buyer clients, a report ordered on a property they're considering demonstrates your commitment to protecting their interests. For seller clients, it demonstrates your commitment to maximizing theirs.
