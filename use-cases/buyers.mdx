Before you commit to one of the largest financial decisions of your life, you deserve to know exactly what a property is worth — not just what the seller is asking. Estify AI gives you an independent, AI-powered valuation backed by data from 76 sources and verified by a certified appraiser, so you can walk into every negotiation with facts, not guesses.

## The Problem: Buying Blind in a Complex Market

Polish real estate markets move fast, and sellers naturally list at optimistic prices. Without independent data, you face real risks:

- **Overpaying** by tens of thousands of złoty simply because you lacked a reference point
- **Missing legal issues** buried in the Księga Wieczysta (land and mortgage register) that could cost you later
- **Ignoring neighborhood trends** that affect whether a property will hold or lose value over time
- **Negotiating without leverage** — relying on gut feel instead of certified market evidence

Most buyers only discover these problems after signing. Estify helps you discover them before.

## How Estify AI Helps Buyers

Estify delivers a comprehensive property report by the next business day. You fill in a ~30-field online form, the AI processes data from 76 sources — including comparable sales, zoning records, environmental databases, and macroeconomic indicators — and a certified appraiser verifies the result before it reaches your inbox.

As a buyer, this means you get:

- An **independent valuation figure** to compare against the asking price
- A **legal verification** of the property's status in the Księga Wieczysta
- **Neighborhood and location analysis** so you understand what you're buying into
- **Price forecasts up to 20 quarters** so you know where values are likely heading
- **Competition analysis** showing how comparable properties are priced right now

## Your Step-by-Step Buyer Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a Property You're Serious About">
    Identify a property you want to pursue — ideally one where you're ready to move toward a preliminary agreement (umowa przedwstępna). The earlier you order the report in the process, the more leverage you have.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order Your Estify Report">
    Complete the online form with the property's details: address, size, floor, condition, and other relevant characteristics. The form takes about 15 minutes. Choose the **Pro plan** for full analysis including forecasts and investment data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare the Asking Price to the AI Valuation">
    When your report arrives the next business day, the first number to check is the **valuation figure**. Compare it directly to what the seller is asking. Any significant gap is your opening for negotiation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the Legal Verification Section">
    Check the legal section for any encumbrances, mortgages, easements, or irregularities in the Księga Wieczysta. Issues flagged here could affect the property's title or future resale — and give you grounds to renegotiate or walk away.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze the Price Forecast">
    Review the 20-quarter price forecast to understand whether the market in this location is expected to appreciate, stabilize, or soften. This context shapes what a "fair price" looks like over a 3–5 year horizon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Negotiate With Data">
    Enter negotiations armed with the Estify valuation, comparable market data, and forecast trends. Instead of saying "I think it's worth less," you can say "According to an appraiser-verified AI analysis, the market value is X."
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Real-World Scenario: Apartment in Warsaw, Asking Price 750,000 PLN

Imagine you find a 60 m² apartment in a well-located Warsaw district listed at **750,000 PLN**. The photos look great, the agent is enthusiastic, and you're tempted to move quickly.

You order an Estify Pro report. The next morning, you learn:

- The **AI valuation comes in at 710,000 PLN** — a 40,000 PLN gap versus the asking price
- The competition analysis shows three comparable apartments in the same district sold for between 695,000 and 720,000 PLN in the last quarter
- The price forecast shows moderate growth expected over the next 8 quarters, but no urgency to overpay today
- The legal check reveals a historical mortgage notation that has since been discharged — no issue, but worth confirming with a notary

Armed with this data, you approach the seller with a counter-offer of 715,000 PLN backed by the report. The negotiation is productive, evidence-based, and — critically — you don't overpay by 35,000 PLN.
